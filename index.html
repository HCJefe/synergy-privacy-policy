Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

SYNERGY HomeCare of Yuma, Arizona (operated by "Hank and Oliver" LLC) built the SYNERGY Caregiver app as a tool for our home health caregivers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

01

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account & Authentication Data — Your login credentials used to authenticate with AxisCare, our scheduling and care management platform.
  • Location Data — GPS coordinates collected at the time of clock-in and clock-out to support Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) compliance.
  • Schedule & Shift Information — Your assigned shifts, client care plans, and related scheduling data retrieved from AxisCare.
  • Device Information — Device identifiers and push notification tokens used to deliver schedule alerts and team communications.
  • Usage Analytics — Anonymous usage data such as app feature usage, session duration, and error reports to help us improve app performance and reliability.
02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate you securely with our care management platform
  • Display your schedule, shift details, and client care plans
  • Enable GPS-verified clock-in and clock-out for EVV compliance
  • Send push notifications for schedule updates, reminders, and team messages
  • Provide access to training resources and team communication tools
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
03

Location Data & Electronic Visit Verification

Location data is collected only at the moment you clock in or clock out of a shift. The app does not track your location continuously or in the background.

GPS-based Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) is required by federal and state regulations to confirm that home health services are delivered at the correct location. Your location data is transmitted to AxisCare solely for EVV compliance and is not used for any other purpose.

04

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • AxisCare — Our home care management platform that stores scheduling, client, and EVV data. Your data is subject to AxisCare's own privacy policy and data handling practices.
  • Expo / React Native — The development framework used to build the app. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and performance data. For details, see Expo's Privacy Policy.
  • Push Notification Services — Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and/or Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) are used to deliver notifications to your device.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

05

Data Retention & Security

We retain your data only for as long as it is needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations, including state and federal EVV recordkeeping requirements.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure token-based authentication
  • Access controls limiting data to authorized personnel

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

06

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Revoke location permissions through your device settings (note: this may prevent EVV clock-in/out functionality)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

07

Children's Privacy

The SYNERGY Caregiver app is designed for use by employed caregivers and is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
